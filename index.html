Understand Human Disease

WE DEVELOP ANALYTICAL METHODS TO TACKLE A DIVERSE SET OF PROBLEMS IN HUMAN GENETIC DISEASE. THE DATASETS ARE PREDOMINANTLY BASED ON MASSIVELY PARALLEL SEQUENCING, AND INCLUDE WHOLE GENOME AND EXOME SEQUENCING, TRANSCRIPTOME SEQUENCING, CHIPSEQ, TRANSCRIPTOMIC AND METHYLATION DATA. WE APPLY VARIOUS GENOMICS AND BIOINFORMATICS APPROACHES TO MENDELIAN DISEASE, POLYGENIC DISEASE, AND CANCER. MEMBERS OF OUR LAB ACTIVELY COLLABORATE WITH CLINICIANS AT MCGILL, THROUGHOUT CANADA, AND WORLDWIDE.
